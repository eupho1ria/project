Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba


Author: Gotoge, Koyoharu
Country: Japan
Release: 6 april,2019
Genres: Adventure Fiction, Dark Fantasy, Martial Arts

Tanjiro Kamado is a young boy who sells charcoal to make a living for his family. One fateful day, his entire family, except for his sister, was killed by a demon, plunging him into a world of fear and despair. After an encounter with a demon-slaying swordsman, Tanjiro and his sister, now a demon herself, must find a way to end the curse and track down the monster that killed their family.

Breath styles

  • Beast Breathing is a distant relative of Breath of the Wind.
  • Insect Breathing is a sword fighting style developed for people that lack in strength.
  • Mist Breathing is a technique that has been solely mastered by Muichiro Tokito.
  • Serpent Breathing is derived from the Breath of Water.
  • Sound Breathing requires its user to have the best hearing capabilities.
  • Stone Breathing grants exceptional levels of dexterity, strength, durability, endurance, and agility.
  • Thunder Breathing is One of the five foundational breathing styles derived from Breath of the Sun.
  • Water Breathing is the most proliferated breathing technique for the same reason.
  • Wind Breathing s the most erratic and unpredictable of all the breathing styles.

Dictionary

Breathing Styles (呼こ吸きゅう法ほう Kokyū Hō?) are swordsmanship styles practiced and taught by members of the Demon Slayer Corps.
The Hashira (柱はしら Hashira, lit. Pillars) are the highest ranking combatants in the Demon Slayer Corps. As such, they are the organization's most elite and powerful warriors who each serve a pivotal role in their war against Muzan Kibutsuji and the Twelve Kizuki.
Demons (鬼おに Oni?) are an ancient race of monstrous, supernatural, immortal beings and the main antagonists of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba.
The Demon Slayer Mark (鬼き殺さつ痣あざ Kisatsu Aza?) is a mysterious marking that can be unlocked and may appear on the body of a strong Demon Slayer.
The Demon Slayer Corps (鬼き殺さつ隊たい Kisatsutai?) is an organization that has existed since ancient times, dedicating its existence to protecting humanity from demons.
